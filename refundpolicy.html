Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping with us. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help. Please read our refund policy below:

Refund Conditions

  • Refunds can only be issued within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • The item must be in its original condition and packaging to be eligible for a refund.
  • If the product is damaged, please provide a detailed explanation and photographs of the damage.
  • Customized or personalized items are not eligible for refunds unless they arrive damaged or defective.
  • If your refund request is approved, we will initiate the refund to your original method of payment.

How to Request a Refund

  • Contact our customer service team at shopspherestorecontact@gmail.com with your order number and reason for the refund.
  • We may ask for additional information or documentation to process your request.

Shipping Costs

Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable. If your refund request is approved, you will receive the full product cost, but any shipping fees paid are not refundable.

Exceptions

Refunds may not be granted in the following situations:

  • If the product has been used or opened (unless damaged upon arrival).
  • If the product is not in its original packaging.
  • If the refund request is made after the 30-day period.

If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact our customer service team. We are here to assist you!
